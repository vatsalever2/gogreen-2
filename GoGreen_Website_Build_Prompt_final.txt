── SERVICE 1: Solar EPC ────────────────────────────────────────────────────

GoGreen provides full-scope Solar EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and 
Construction) services for commercial, distributed generation, and residential 
projects. They manage from initial planning and system design through 
procurement, installation, and final commissioning.

With a strong in-house engineering and electrical team, they ensure seamless 
coordination between design and field execution. Focus on precision, safety, and 
build quality — systems built to perform reliably long after commissioning.

Clients: Commercial building owners, developers, asset owners, EPCs, 
         homeowners.

Project Scale:
  Residential : 5 kW – 25 kW
  Commercial  : 100 kW – 1.5 MW+

── SERVICE 2: Battery Storage & Energy Systems (BESS) ─────────────────────

GoGreen designs and installs battery energy storage systems (BESS) for 
commercial and residential applications — enhancing reliability, providing 
backup power, and optimizing energy usage.

Their team handles system sizing, inverter integration, load calculations, and 
code-compliant installation to ensure seamless operation with solar PV systems.
Use cases: resilience, peak load management, energy independence.

── SERVICE 3: Decommissioning (DCom) ──────────────────────────────────────

Professional solar decommissioning for commercial and residential systems that 
need to be removed, upgraded, or replaced. GoGreen handles safe disconnection, 
dismantling, and removal of solar equipment while maintaining site integrity and 
minimizing disruption.

Focus on proper labeling, documentation, and coordination so systems can be 
reinstalled or repurposed efficiently. Suitable for system upgrades, roof work, 
or asset transitions.

── SERVICE 4: Removal & Repower (R&R) ─────────────────────────────────────

Complete removal and repower services for commercial and residential solar 
systems — for roof replacement, system upgrades, or performance improvements. 
GoGreen manages the full process from removal to reinstallation.

Ensures proper system layout, wiring integrity, and code compliance during 
reinstallation. Close coordination with roofing contractors, asset owners, and 
homeowners to minimize downtime.

── ADDITIONAL SERVICES ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────

- Electrical services: service upgrades, panel replacements, feeder 
  installations, and system tie-ins
- System diagnostics, troubleshooting, and performance optimization
- Operations & Maintenance (O&M): inspections, preventive maintenance, repairs
- Solar system commissioning support and AHJ/utility coordination
- In-house roofing division: assessments, replacements, and solar-integrated 
  roofing for long-term performance
